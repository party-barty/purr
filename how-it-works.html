How Purrfect Match Works

Find your feline soulmate in just a few simple steps!

For Cat Lovers

1

Create Your Profile

Tell us about yourself! Share your lifestyle, living situation, and what you're looking for in a feline companion.

Profile Tips:

  • Be honest about your availability
  • Mention any pet experience
  • Describe your ideal cat personality
2

Swipe & Match

Browse through profiles of adorable adoptable cats in your area. Swipe right if you're interested, left to pass.

Pro Tip:

Read the bios carefully - they reveal each cat's unique personality!

3

Meet Your Match

When it's a match, we'll connect you with the shelter to arrange a meet-and-greet. The adoption process begins!

Note:

Shelters may have additional screening questions before finalizing adoptions.

For Shelters & Rescues

1

Register Your Shelter

Create an account for your organization and verify your 501(c)(3) status to join our network.

Requirements:

  • Proof of nonprofit status
  • Adoption policies
  • Veterinary references
2

Create Cat Profiles

Add your adoptable cats with photos and engaging bios that showcase their personalities.

Profile Tips:

  • Use high-quality photos
  • Highlight unique traits
  • Be honest about special needs
  • Add fun facts (e.g., "Loves belly rubs!")
3

Connect with Adopters

Review matches, communicate with potential adopters, and schedule meet-ups through our platform.

Features:

  • Messaging system
  • Adoption application forms
  • Meet-up scheduling
