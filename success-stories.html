Sarah & Mochi's Story

From Sarah's Perspective:

"After losing my senior cat, I wasn't sure I was ready for another pet. Purrfect Match helped me find Mochi, a shy 2-year-old who needed extra patience. The bio mentioned she loved feather toys but was scared of loud noises - perfect for my quiet apartment! Our first meeting at the shelter was magical. She hid at first, but when I dangled a feather toy, she couldn't resist. Now she sleeps curled up next to me every night."

From Mochi's Perspective:

At first I wasn't sure about this human - they're so big and loud! But she brought my favorite toy and didn't try to pet me until I was ready. Now I have the best nap spots, regular meals, and someone who understands when I need space. 10/10 would recommend this human.

Sarah K.
